Events

SWIFT holds events for people of all skill levels. Whether it's our flagship competition Red vs. Blue, a home-made CTF, or a casual social with pizza in PJs, theres always a way for you to get involved in the SWIFT community. We also host an annual student-run conference, Tech Symposium, opening previously-closed doors for students who are new to cybersecurity. We also assist Bronco Esports with a semi-annual on-campus LAN gaming event called FragFest by hosting the networking infrastructure for gamers. If you'd like to collaborate with us or just join in on the fun, contact us below.
